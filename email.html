Premier Yarns looking for Passionate Knitters and Crocheters
Hello!

Premier Yarns is looking for passionate knitters and crocheters to participate in local consumer focus groups in the Charlotte, North Carolina metro area in April.

We are looking for in-person consumer feedback as we move through the process of rebranding our popular anti-pill yarn collection, Deborah Norville Everyday.

By taking our short online survey (3 minutes or less) you can share your knitting and crocheting insights with us as well as sign up for the opportunity to participate in an upcoming focus group.

Your feedback is invaluable to us! To show our appreciation, every selected focus group participant who physically attends will receive a free goody bag filled with surprises, including our anti-pill Deborah Norville Everyday yarn.

We look forward to hearing from you.

Thank you and keep crafting!
